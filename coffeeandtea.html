Freshly Roasted Coffee and Flavorful Tea

Start your morning the right way by enjoying a hot cup of freshly roasted coffee. At The Coffee Trade, preparing coffee is what we do best. We are a locally owned business with coffee from the four corners of the world, bringing you an incredible beverage along with the finest customer service. Our coffee is roasted on site, and we capture joy and spice in each and every cup of coffee we brew.

We don’t just serve coffee fanatics; we welcome tea drinkers, as well! Shop for tea bags or loose leaf tea at our shop. We have a wide selection of caffeinated and decaffeinated tea for you to choose from.

Roasting On Site Since 1989

The Coffee Trade has been serving Farmington, West Hartford, Granby, Avon, Simsbury, Canton, and the surrounding Connecticut areas since 1989. Whether you come by for coffee and tea or to purchase mugs, we have you covered. Visit us soon to see the 80 varieties of coffee beans we offer.
