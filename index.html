30 Years of Serving Joy in Every Cup

80 Varieties of Delicious, Freshly Roasted Coffee

At The Coffee Trade, you can expect every cup of coffee to be high-quality, fresh, and locally prepared. We have been Avon's only coffee shop offering freshly roasted coffee since 1989.

The coffee beans roasted in our shop have been imported from the four corners of the world and are freshly roasted in our very own roaster! For an unbeatable cup of coffee, visit The Coffee Trade in Avon, CT.

Coffee, Tea, Mugs, and So Much More

The Coffee Trade serves coffee and tea drinkers in Farmington, West Hartford, Granby, Avon, Simsbury, Canton, and the surrounding Connecticut areas. Purchase everything you need for an enjoyable coffee or tea experience, including coffee beans, tea bags and loose tea, coffee mugs, antique tea sets, and so much more. Not only do we serve up fresh coffee, but we make sure you have what you need to bring The Coffee Trade experience home. Stop by soon to take a look at our selection.
